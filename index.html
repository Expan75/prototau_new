Introduction


PrototAU is a team comprised of engineering & business students that together help contruct a hydrogen powered vechicle from scratch, all for the purpose of competing in the Shell Eco-marathon Europe 2019.

The contest, being held this summer, is a massive international event where teams from all over the world compete to see who can produce the most efficient and clean racing vechicle. By utilising hydrogen fuel cells, our car will be able to navigate the track with the only exhaust being water.

There is a lot to be done, and our work as only started.
